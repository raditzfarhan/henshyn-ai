# Validate Idea

## Overview
Stress-test an idea to find fatal flaws before investing time in it. Forces specificity at every step — no vague answers allowed.

## Process
1. **Define the target user** — who specifically has this problem? Not "everyone" or "small businesses". Name a real persona.
2. **Problem clarity** — is this a real, recurring pain? Or a nice-to-have? Evidence?
3. **Market exists** — who else is solving this? (Competitors are validation.) How big is the space?
4. **Top 3 kill risks** — what would make this fail? Be specific: "no one will pay for this because X", not "competitive market".
5. **Verdict** — Proceed / Pivot / Kill + one sentence of rationale.

## Output Format
Each step as a section. Verdict is bold and on its own line.
