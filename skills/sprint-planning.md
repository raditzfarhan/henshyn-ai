# Skill: Sprint Planning

## Purpose
Plan a focused, achievable week of work from the backlog of ideas and tasks.

## Process
1. **Pull active work** — read `memory/ideas-log.md`, `memory/decisions-log.md`, and any recent task outputs. What's in flight?
2. **Filter by priority** — what is blocking progress? What has the highest near-term impact?
3. **Fit into hours** — estimate total available hours for the week. Be realistic (not 40h if it's 25h). Fit only what fits.
4. **Define daily focus** — group tasks into daily themes or blocks. One big thing per day.
5. **Set done criteria** — for the sprint as a whole: what does "good week" look like?

## Output Format
Follow the task-manager agent output structure. Add a "Sprint Goal" section at the top: one sentence describing the week's theme.
